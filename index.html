Marketing Calculators

COS and ROAS Calculator

This tool provides a comprehensive way to calculate the Cost of Sales (COS) by factoring in material costs, labor expenses, and operational overheads. It helps businesses assess the efficiency of their spending and optimize their Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).

Hypothetical COS and Budget Change Calculator

Explore how changes in your budget impact the overall financial picture with this calculator. It allows you to simulate variations in the Cost of Sales (COS) and evaluate their effects on profitability, enabling better-informed pricing and financial strategy decisions.

Incrementality Test Length Calculator

Ensure accurate measurement of your marketing efforts with this tool. It helps determine the ideal length for an incrementality test, providing enough data to make statistically significant, data-driven decisions on campaign effectiveness.
